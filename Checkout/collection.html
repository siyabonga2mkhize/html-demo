Collection

Woolworths Collection Policy

1. Purpose

This policy outlines the procedures and guidelines for customers collecting orders from Woolworths stores. It ensures a smooth, secure, and efficient collection process, maintaining high customer service standards.


2. Collection Methods

Customers can choose from the following collection options:

  • In-Store Collection: Collect from the customer service desk at a selected Woolworths store.
  • Curbside Collection: Park in a designated area, and a staff member will bring the order to your vehicle (available at select locations).
  • Locker Collection: Pick up from secure lockers available at specific stores (available 24/7 at participating stores).

3. Collection Timeframes

  • Orders are typically ready for collection within:
    • 2–6 hours for same-day orders (if placed before the daily cutoff).
    • Next day for orders placed after the cutoff time.
  • Customers will receive an SMS and/or email notification once the order is ready.

4. Collection Requirements

To collect an order, the customer must present:

  • A valid order confirmation (SMS or email).
  • A valid photo ID (e.g. driver’s license, ID book, passport).
  • If someone else is collecting on the customer’s behalf, they must bring:
    • A copy of the customer’s ID,
    • Their own ID, and
    • The order confirmation.

5. Holding Period

  • Orders will be held for 7 calendar days from the date of notification.
  • Uncollected orders after this period may be:
    • Cancelled and refunded (excluding perishable items), or
    • Returned to inventory.
  • Perishable items not collected within 24 hours may be discarded with no refund due to health and safety regulations.

6. Changes & Cancellations

  • Customers may change their collection time or location (if available) up to 2 hours before the selected collection window.
  • To cancel an order, contact Woolworths Customer Service at least 2 hours before the collection time.

7. Returns at Collection

  • Customers may review their order during collection.
  • If any item is incorrect or damaged, customers can request an immediate return or exchange at the collection point.
